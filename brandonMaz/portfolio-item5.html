ios wallpaper

React Native

.

When thinking of an idea for my very first ios app, I was thinking simple, yet fun. I have always enjoyed the idea of a wallpaper app and have used a few myself. This was the first time that I had used React Native, thinking it would be very similar to React. After a week of teaching myself react, I found out that it was similar in some ways and completely different in others. I enjoyed working with react native and plan on doing a couple more projects in this language. I plan on refining my wallpaper app to be a little more customizable and user friendl before I try and deploy it to ios. My goal with this wallpaper app is to be tailored towards the artist, thier profile and the location of the image, along with the description of the location.
